Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper until well mixed. You can add water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Heat a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to spread them evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Add your chosen fillings once the eggs start to set but are still slightly runny.
  5. Fold and serve: Carefully lift one side of the omelette and fold it over the fillings.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with extra salt and pepper if needed.
